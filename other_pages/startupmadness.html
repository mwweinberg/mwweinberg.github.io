It's Almost Time for Legal Madness
Join the Engelberg Center for an Open Mic Night where lawyers tell their harrowing tales from inside tech companies and startups.
Nov 18, 2019
Doors are at 6, Show is at 7
Attorneys. Anonymous Stories. Actors. Alcohol. What's the worst that could happen?
Working in a tech company can be madness, and lawyers at those companies have a front row seat. In house attorneys have tales to tell, but confidentiality obligations have kept them silent.
Until now....
The Engelberg Center on Innovation Law & Policy has collected anonymous stories from lawyers inside tech companies. Actors will play the role of tech lawyers at an open mic night in the Village.
Join hosts Sarah Feingold and Michael Weinberg for an evening of . . . something.
